Our Story

Born from
Passion & Thread

Umalaya began as a small home studio with a single roll of thread and a dream to keep traditional Telugu craft alive. Every piece we make honours the artisan in all of us.

We source our raw materials, work with locals, and ensure that each product reaching you carries the warmth of handmade authenticity. No machines. No shortcuts. Only craft.
